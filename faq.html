General Ordering Payment Timing Usage & Rights Queue

GENERAL

What programs do you use?

I use base Krita! I don't use any extra addons or downloaded brushes.

How long have you been drawing / taking commissions?

I've been drawing for almost all my life! I used to be very active on the Amino platform back in 2014–2019, and I've been taking commissions through Discord since 2021.

What types of commissions do you offer?

You can check out all my commission options on my Commissions Page! I pretty much offer whatever I'm able to do.

Do you draw fanart / OCs / furry / humanoids / etc.?

Yep! I draw both fanart and OCs regardless of species. While I'm most experienced with humans and humanoids, I can also draw ferals, monsters, and other creatures.

What won't you draw?

Most of my “Don'ts” are listed in my Terms Of Service and on the main Commissions Page. In general, I avoid drawing anything that makes me uncomfortable.

Are your commissions for personal use only?

Yes, unless stated otherwise! Commercial-use artwork follows different terms and legalities, all of which are explained in my ToS.

What's your favorite thing to draw?

I love drawing kemonomimis— my main OC is a snake kemonomimi!!

I'm also a huge fan of Cybercore and Cyberpunk aesthetics.

Do you take art trades/collabs?

I do take art trades and collabs! Just keep in mind there's no guarantee I'll finish them quickly.

Do you accept customs/design work?

Nope! I haven't made pricing for customs yet since character design isn't really my favorite thing to do.

Ordering Process

How do I order a commission?

You can order a commission by contacting me directly, explaining what you'd like, and sending references of your character! The full process is explained in my ToS.

Can I commission you without a reference sheet?

While you don't necessarily need a full reference sheet, I do need clear references of your character using flat colors only (no heavy rendering or shading).

I also won't accept commissions where important details are missing. For example, if your only reference is a half-body drawing, I can't accurately draw a full-body version. I'm not a character designer.

Can I request changes during the process?

Yes! You can request changes throughout the process. However, major changes requested after the lineart phase may require an extra fee.

Do you send sketches/WIPs?

I do! I try to share as many WIPs as possible throughout the process so we both stay on the same page.

Can I post the commissioned art?

Of course— once it's fully finished! I'd prefer if WIPs aren't reposted publicly, though. Credit is appreciated, but not required.

Can I commission matching art with a friend/partner?

Yep! Just keep in mind that the price will increase due to the additional character(s).

Do you redesign characters or only draw existing designs?

I only draw existing character designs— that's why I ask for clear references beforehand.

Can you work from text descriptions?

Not when it comes to designing the character itself. However, I can work from text descriptions for poses, expressions, or background ideas, though visual references are still greatly appreciated!

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

PayPal, Ko-fi, and Patreon.

Do you require payment upfront?

I require 50% of the payment upfront, and the remaining 50% before the final artwork is delivered.

Can I pay in installments?

For now, no. However, I can mark you down as a possible commissioner until you're able to gather the money you need!

Are refunds available?

Refunds are rare, especially after I've already started or completed the artwork. More information can be found in my ToS.

Do prices vary depending on complexity?

Prices vary depending on the type of commission you request. For example, a Sketch Headshot won't cost the same as a Fully Rendered Full Body piece.

Character complexity itself does not affect the price.

Timing

How long do commissions take?

Commissions usually take around 2 weeks to complete. However, real-life situations may extend this up to a month maximum.

If a delay becomes too long, I may offer either:

  • a cancellation/refund option, or
  • to finish the artwork without requiring the remaining 50% payment.

    • Do you offer rush commissions?

    Yep! Rush commissions are available for an additional fee.

    What happens if you need extra time?

    I'll always let you know beforehand! I'll explain the reason for the delay and show you the current progress of your commission.

    Usage & Rights

    Can I use the art as a profile picture/banner?

    Of course!

    Can I print the commission?

    Yep!

    Can I use it commercially?

    Only if the commission was purchased as Commercial Use Artwork. Additional legal terms and agreements will apply.

    Will the commission have a watermark?

    Only a small signature overlay on the artwork.

    Will you post the artwork publicly?

    Most likely, yes! I may use it as portfolio/reference material on my website, post it on my socials, or share it in servers I'm in.

    Queue / Availability

    Are your commissions currently open?

    There's an indicator for that on the main Commissions Page!

    Do you work first-come first-served?

    Mostly, yes! Priority depends mainly on who paid first.

    Rush commissions will always take priority, though I usually work on multiple commissions at the same time.

    Do you have a public queue or Trello?

    I don't publicly display my queue. However, I will give you an approximate wait time before I start working on your commission.
